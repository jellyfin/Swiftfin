# GuamaFlix Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 27, 2026

GuamaFlix ("the app", "we", "us") is a media player for Apple TV that connects to a
Jellyfin media server that **you** choose and control. This policy explains what
information the app handles and how. In short: **GuamaFlix does not collect, sell, or
share your personal data.** The app talks only to the server you configure.

---

## Who we are

GuamaFlix is developed by guama.dev. You can reach us at **guamaflix@guama.dev**.

GuamaFlix is an independent, third-party client for Jellyfin. It is **not** affiliated
with, endorsed by, or operated by the Jellyfin project.

---

## Information the app handles

### Information you provide
To use GuamaFlix you enter the address of your Jellyfin server and sign in with the
username and password (or Quick Connect code) for that server. This information is:

- **Stored locally on your device** (credentials and access tokens are kept in the
  device Keychain).
- **Sent only to the Jellyfin server you specify**, in order to sign in, browse your
  library, and play media.

We do not receive, transmit, or store your server address, credentials, or media
activity on any GuamaFlix server. We do not operate a backend service.

### Information sent to your server
When you use the app, normal client activity (such as which items you browse, playback
position, and "watched" status) is sent to **your** Jellyfin server so it can provide
those features. How your server stores and uses that information is governed by the
operator of that server, not by GuamaFlix.

### Diagnostic logs
The app can keep technical logs on your device to help diagnose problems (visible in
the in-app Console). These logs stay **on your device** and are not automatically sent
to us. If you choose to share a log with us for support, you control what you send.

### Analytics and tracking
GuamaFlix contains **no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs**. We do
not track you across apps or websites, and we do not build advertising profiles.

---

## How information is shared

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone. Data leaves
your device only to **the Jellyfin server you configure**, in order to provide the app's
core functionality.

---

## Data retention and deletion

Because GuamaFlix stores your information locally, you can remove it at any time by
signing out (which clears stored credentials and tokens) or by deleting the app, which
removes its locally stored data from your device. Any data held by your Jellyfin server
is controlled by that server's operator.

---

## Children's privacy

GuamaFlix is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal
information from children. The content available in the app comes from the server you
connect to and is controlled by that server's operator.

---

## Security

Connections to your server use the protocol your server provides (HTTP or HTTPS).
We recommend configuring your Jellyfin server with HTTPS. Credentials are stored using
the device Keychain. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we
design the app to keep your information on your device and your server only.

updating the "Effective date" above. Continued use of the app after an update
